About EventSphere

A platform for finding college fests, hackathons, and workshops.

OUR MISSION

Helping Students Find Campus Events

We believe college learning happens everywhere—in hackathons, sports meets, cultural fests, and workshops.

Our goal is to make it easy for student clubs to share their events, and for students from any college to find and register for them in one place.

OUR VISION

Better Student Collaboration

We want to make it easier for students from different backgrounds to participate in regional events, whether it's technology, sports, or arts.
